Liquid City Website

The website is the navigation menu and menu is the website, depending on how far you expand and collapse it. All effects guide user behavior by playing with alignment: rotation and chromatic aberration effects are undone when the content hits the center of the screen. On mobile, the site uses the phone orientation to aberrate the images in the direction of gravity.
