What is Guiville?

Guiville is a city within a graphical user interface. All the roads and houses are live on the web and may be moved by anyone, which is a HUGE inconvenience, because sometimes a citizen might find that all roads to their house or work may be moved (or worse, their entire house).

It is currently an election year in Guiville, and the citizens are voting about getting rid of their monarchy. There are protests, because the ballot center is just a folder, and anyone on the internet can click on a ballot and view how a citizen voted. The political unrest in 2D Guiville has prompted the wealthy citizens to pursue the production of St. Guis, a 3D city. The entry point to St. Guis sits beyond the Great Paywall, and all property there is constrained by a polygon quota.

You may move the city by dragging. Double click to enlarge an element.
